The Gentlemen

Christopher Eleiott - Best Man

Chris is Jon's closest friend in Chicago. Despite only meeting a little over a year ago, they hit it off right away when Jon found out that Chris's ideal drink to have with a fancy dinner was the same as his: chocolate milk. A true character out of "Up In The Air", Chris's job takes him all over the country and despite all this, he remains Jon's most trusted confidant.

Hyesung Oh - Groomsman

Hyesung "Hace" Oh is Jon's oldest and best friend from Northwestern University. Having gone through all the peaks and valleys of growing in the Christian faith and the college experience together, each time they get together feels like they can pick up from where they left off last despite being separated by many miles. A modern-day Richard Aoki, Hace motivates Jon everyday to stand firm in what he believes in.

Kun Chen - Groomsman

Kun is Jon's most unlikely close friend. After sharing a common romantic history, Jon and Kun's initial meeting was with much tension and some might even say, mild disdain. Despite the odds, Jon and Kun's friendship grew over the years through studying Christ together and of course, showing each other up on the frisbee field. Though Kun is miles away as a medical student in New York City, he continues to be one of Jon's closest friends.

Dr. John Lee - Groomsman

Jon's life has been immeasurably heightened having been friends with Dr. Lee for many years. Dr. Lee personifies what it means to be a genuine friend by constantly checking in on Jon and ensuring his well-being. In spite of being separated by thousands of miles and having not seen each other in years, Dr. Lee was the very first person Jon asked to be a groomsman for the wedding.

Dr. Charles Santilukka - Groomsman

Jon first met Dr. Santilukka at a small group Bible study several years ago and did not care for him upon their first meeting. This quickly changed as Jon spent more time with him - he and Lexi even attended Dr. Santilukka's wedding! Dr. Santilukka has proven time and time again to be a divinely inspired voice of reason in Jon's life and continues to be one of Jon's most trusted spiritual mentors and teachers.
